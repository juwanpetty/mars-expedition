About Us.

Space's vison: We reach for new heights and reveal the unknown for the benefit
of humankind.

To do that, thousands of people have been working around the world -- and off
of it -- for more than 50 years, trying to answer some basic questions. What's
out there in space? How do we get there? What will we find? What can we learn
there, or learn just by trying to get there, that will make life better here on Earth?
