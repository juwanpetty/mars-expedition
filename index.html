Journey to Mars Overview

  1. Earth Reliant

    International Space Station operation
    through 2024

    Commercial development of low-Earth
    orbit

    Development of deep space systems,
    life support and human health

  2. Proving Ground

    Regular crewed missons and spacewalks
    in cislunar space

    Verify deep space habitatoin and conduct
    a yearlong mision to validate readiness
    for mars

    Demonstration integrated human and robotic
    operations by redirecting and sampling an
    asteriod boulder

  3. Earth Independent

    Science missions pave the way to Mars

    Demonstrate entry, descent, and landing
    and in-situ resource use

    Conduct robotic round-trip demonstrations
    with sample return in the late 2020s

    Send humans to orbit Mars in the early 2030s

Mars One Mission

NASA is on a journey to Mars, with a
goal of sending humans to the Red Planet
in the 2030s. That journey is already
well under way.
